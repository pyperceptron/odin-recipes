Pasta Salad

Description

Simple salad made from fresh ingredients ideal for a healthy snack.

Ingredients

  • Pasta, 200g, preferably fusilli, but any other short sized form will be fine.
  • Salad Tomatoes, 10
  • Olives, black (6), green (6)
  • Cucumber, 1
  • Onion, 1
  • Olive Oil
  • Feta Cheese, 50g
  • Salt and Pepper

Preparation

  1. Cook the pasta with plenty of water.
  2. Drain the pasta and add a touch of olive oil. Stir.
  3. Chop the Cheese, Olives, Tomatoes, Onion and Cucummber into small bits.
  4. Mix everything and add salt and pepper.
  5. Enjoy.
