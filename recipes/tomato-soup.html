Tomato Soup

Description

Hearty, simple and nutritious soup.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes, 6.
  • Garlic, one full kernel
  • Vegetable stock cubes, 2
  • Cucumber, 1
  • Onion, 1
  • Parsley
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Preparation

  1. Chop the tomatoes, onion, garlic and cucumber, sprinkle some olive oil, and put in a pre-heated oven.
  2. When the vegetables are ready, take them out of the oven and into a cooking pot on medium heat. Add boiling water just enough to cover.
  3. Add the stock cubes, salt and pepper.
  4. Use a hand blender while cooking. Blend until it is perfectly uniform.
  5. Serve with a touch of evaporated milk and parsley.
  6. Enjoy.
